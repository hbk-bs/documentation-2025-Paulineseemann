Graphic Novel

Assignment

Collaborate with your team to create a graphic novel.

Realisation

Anna, Luca, and I didn’t have a specific story in mind, so we thought it would be interesting to let AI help us create the storyline. Our only input was a description of the three of us in the classroom as a starting point. We then generated pictures step by step with ChatGPT, so the AI had to react to each image to create the content for the next one. In the end, it became a random and actually funny story of us battling a spaghetti monster in different settings. The website was designed specifically for a phone, resembling an Instagram profile, with a new story appearing containing the actual project.
