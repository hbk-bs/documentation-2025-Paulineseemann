Text the Universal Interface

Assignment

Create your own AI, designed to do more than just function as a chatbot.

Realisation

I decided to program an AI that generates a very cheesy lyric phrase for each picture you upload, called “Der Lyrische Bot” (the lyric bot). The idea was to have a simple, understated main page that transforms into an annoyingly pink and glittery page once the picture is loaded. Most of the work lay in the CSS, which required a lot of fine-tuning and adjustments.

Reflection

Unfortunately, I was sick quite often during the time of this assignment, so I understand the critique that my project still stays rather close to Fabian’s template. If I had the chance to do it again, I would aim to create something more complex. Nevertheless, I really enjoyed designing the website’s visuals and developing the idea overall, and I think it’s very interesting to see how the system itself works. One thing that frustrated me a bit was that I wanted to improve the readability of the lyrical line’s font, but for some reason I just couldn’t get it to work the way I intended.
